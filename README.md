## Stores Guider App

**Stores Guider** is a mobile application that showcases a curated list of dining spots in Thessaloniki, Greece. It is designed for visitors, tourists, and locals alike who want quick and easy access to quality food recommendations‚Äîpersonally selected and tested by the app's developers. This app was developed as part of an academic mobile app development course.

The purpose of the app is to centralize these recommendations in one place, avoiding the common issue of forgetting or losing food suggestions seen in social media posts or videos.
